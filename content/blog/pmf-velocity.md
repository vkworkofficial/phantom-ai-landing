# The Death of Traditional QA

In the fast-moving world of YC startups, traditional QA is too slow. By the time you get feedback from a focus group, your competitors have already iterated three times. Manual QA is no longer a safety net; it's a drag co-efficient.

## The Problem with Humans

Traditional beta testing and focus groups suffer from four fatal flaws:
- **Response Bias**: Users tell you what they think you want to hear, not how they actually use the product.
- **Latency**: It takes weeks to recruit, test, and synthesize results.
- **Inconsistency**: Ten different testers will find ten different "bugs" but miss the overall friction.
- **Cost**: High-fidelity human feedback is the most expensive line item in a growth budget.

## The Solution: AI Ghosts
