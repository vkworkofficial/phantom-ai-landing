# Measuring Cognitive Load in UI/UX

## What is Cognitive Load?
The amount of mental energy required for a user to process and interact with a digital interface. High cognitive load directly correlates to funnel drop-off and churn.

## How it is Measured (The Old Way)
Historically, measuring cognitive load required a usability lab with specialized equipment:
1. **Physiological:** Pupillometry (pupil dilation), Eye Tracking (fixations, scan path entropy), Heart Rate Variability, EEG.
2. **Behavioral:** Time on task, task failure rates, "Tapping Method" (disruptions in tapping rhythm).

## The Phantom Advantage
Traditional cognitive load measurement is absurdly expensive and completely detached from the agile shipping cycle. By the time a startup books an eye-tracking lab, they are bankrupt.

## Actionable Phantom Takeaway
**Phantom Ghosts Simulate Cognitive Load.**
