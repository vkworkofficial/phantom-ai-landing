# B2B SaaS Churn & UI/UX Friction

## The Baseline Metrics
- **Average Annual Churn (2025):** The average B2B SaaS churn rate is ~4.9% annually, or ~3.5% monthly.
- **Voluntary vs Involuntary:** 2.6% of this monthly churn is *voluntary* (the user actively decided to leave because the product failed them), while 0.8% is involuntary (credit card failed).

## UX Friction as the Primary Driver
- 88% of users are less likely to return to a site after a single bad experience.
- Complex onboarding processes, lack of perceived value, inefficient navigation, and poor accessibility to customer support are the leading UX-related factors contributing to B2B SaaS churn.
- **The ROI of UX:** Every dollar invested in UX can yield a return of $100.

## Actionable Phantom Takeaway
"Fixing involuntary churn boosts revenue by 8.6%. Fixing VOLUNTARY churn (UX Friction) dictates whether you survive your Series A." 
Phantom specifically targets voluntary churn by ensuring the onboarding and core loops are friction-free before the user ever sees them.
