- In 2024/2025, the median activation rate for B2B SaaS is **37%**.

## The Time-To-Value Threat

- **The Churn Correlator:** Every 10-minute delay in a user experiencing value results in an **8% drop in conversion**. Elite performers are pushing TTV down to under 10 minutes.
