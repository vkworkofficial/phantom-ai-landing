# The Cost of UI Bugs in Production

## Financial Impact
- **The 100x Rule:** Fixing a bug in production costs 10-100x more than addressing it during the design/development phase.
- **Enterprise Downtime:** One hour of system downtime costs enterprises an average of $300,000. 
- **Developer Productivity:** Developers spend ~42% of their time dealing with technical debt and maintenance (including production bug fixes) rather than building new features.

## Churn & Reputational Damage
- **The One-Bug Rule:** 48% of users will abandon an app after encountering just 1 or 2 bugs. 72% abandon after a single bug encounter if the app is easily replaceable.
- Support tickets increase by up to 40% when a critical UI bug reaches production.
