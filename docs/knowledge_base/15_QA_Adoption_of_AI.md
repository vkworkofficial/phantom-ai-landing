# How QA Engineers Use AI (2024-2025 Adoption)

## The Rapid Shift Left
- **Widespread Adoption:** 61% defining themselves as QA teams are adopting AI-driven testing. 89% of organizations are actively piloting Generative AI in quality engineering.
- **Agentic Automation:** The market has moved beyond AI "writing test scripts" to Agentic AI, where the system autonomously reasons about a UI and attempts to fulfill a goal via multimodal vision models.

## The Bottleneck: Trust & Hallucinations
- Despite the rush to adopt, **60% of QA engineers cite AI hallucinations and lack of reliability as their primary concern.** 
- They don't want a "Black Box" that just outputs Pass/Fail. They demand an explainable audit trail. Human engineers want to validate AI-generated reports, not blindly trust them.
