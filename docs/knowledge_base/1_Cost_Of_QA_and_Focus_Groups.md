# Cost of Manual QA & B2B Focus Groups

## General Statistics
- **Offshore Outsourcing:** Manual testing rates can drop to $15-$20 per hour in regions like Vietnam. Eastern Europe sits around $40-$50/hr.
- **US Domestic Rates:** Average hourly pay for a QA Manual Tester is ~$41.52, with top earners reaching $125k annually.
- **SaaS R&D Spend Context:** B2B SaaS companies spend approximately 18% to 20% of their Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) on Research and Development, which QA consumes a significant chunk of.

## The Cost of Beta Testing Cohorts
- B2B user testing and focus groups typically cost upwards of **$2,000 to $10,000+ per cohort**.
- Recruiting, vetting, and managing these manual cohorts takes **weeks of lead time**.
- **Post-Release Costs:** Bugs found post-release cost 15x to 100x more to fix than pre-release bugs (The "Cost of Quality" metric).

## Actionable Phantom Takeaway
Startups are caught between spending $20/hr on a blind offshore test script and $10,000 on a slow human focus group. **Phantom "Ghosts" bridge this gap**, providing the speed and fraction-of-a-penny cost of offshore scripts with the heuristic context of a human tester.
