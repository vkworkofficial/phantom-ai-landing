# Pitching Devtools to Y Combinator

## Unique YC Insights for Devtools
1. **Developer-Led Teams:** 74% of YC dev tool companies have *only* technical co-founders. YC loves founders who are explicitly building for themselves ("You are your audience").
2. **Runtime Ideas Win:** Tools that developers *need* to keep their products running smoothly (like testing and deployment infra) have higher usage potential than nice-to-have dashboard analytics.
3. **Show, Don't Tell:** Devtool pitches must demonstrate the product. The legendary Twilio pitch was just live-coding a phone call. Phantom should focus on *deploying a ghost live* during the pitch.
4. **Bottom-Up Adoption:** Pitch the "land and expand" model. Phantom is adopted by an angry QA engineer first, who then forces the VP of Eng to buy the enterprise tier.

## Actionable Phantom Deck Structure
- **Problem:** "Developers spend 42% of their week fixing production bugs because $10,000 human focus groups are too slow for agile shipping."
- **Solution:** "We inject 500 AI Ghosts into the CI/CD pipeline to break your frontend before your users do."
- **Traction/Secret Sauce:** "We've mapped ML evaluation frameworks to subjective human frustration (cognitive load metrics)."
