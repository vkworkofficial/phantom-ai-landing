# PMF, Iteration Speed, and Feedback Loops

## The Iron Law of Startups
Product Market Fit (PMF) is not a single point in time; it is a moving target. 
- The #1 reason startups die before Series A is the failure to find PMF (because they build solutions based on internal assumptions rather than market demand).
- The only proven mechanism to achieve PMF is the **Feedback Loop**: Build -> Launch -> Gather Feedback -> Adjust -> Repeat.

## Speed is the Only Moat
- Traditional feedback loops (Focus groups, Beta testing cohorts, waiting for Intercom tickets to roll in) are tragically slow. A standard beta cohort takes 3 weeks to organize and execute.
- Startups running 3-week iteration cycles will be outcompeted by startups running 3-hour iteration cycles. Iteration velocity determines survivor rates.
